# 477 - Org Chart & Directory Tool

**Date:** 2026-05-02

## Overview

Org chart and directory tools provide visual representations of organisational structure alongside searchable employee profiles, reporting-line data, and people analytics. The core value is maintaining an accurate, always-current view of a workforce—something that becomes increasingly difficult to sustain manually as organisations grow, restructure, or operate across multiple geographies. Modern platforms go well beyond static diagram generation, offering live synchronisation with HRIS systems, headcount planning, diversity and inclusion analytics, and scenario modelling for reorgs. The primary buyers are HR, People Operations, and Finance teams, though the directory component serves all employees as a day-to-day reference tool.

## Market Landscape

ChartHop is the most feature-complete people-analytics platform in the segment, combining dynamic org charts with real-time HRIS sync (Workday, BambooHR, ADP, HiBob, UKG, Deel), scenario-based headcount planning, compensation review tools, and DEI analytics in one system. Lucidchart is the most widely used diagramming tool with robust org chart capabilities, but it requires manual re-import of employee data to stay current—a meaningful administrative burden compared with purpose-built tools. Workleap Pingboard focuses on the interactive employee directory and org chart use case for mid-sized organisations, with clean UX and HRIS sync. Organimi targets smaller organisations with an easy-to-use, template-driven org chart builder. Microsoft Visio and Miro serve general diagramming needs and can produce org charts but lack people-data integration. Rippling and other HCM platforms include org chart functionality as a bundled feature, making standalone tools harder to justify for organisations already on a full HCM.

## Key Features / Tools

1. **Live HRIS sync** — bi-directional or read-sync connection to Workday, BambooHR, HiBob, ADP, Rippling, Deel, and Personio so org charts update automatically when people join, move, or leave
2. **Interactive visual org chart** — zoomable, searchable hierarchy with click-through to employee profiles, department filters, and alternate views (flat, matrix, divisional)
3. **Employee directory and profiles** — searchable database with photos, contact details, role, team, location, skills, and social links
4. **Headcount planning and scenario modelling** — draft future-state org structures, model budget impact of proposed reorgs, and compare scenarios before committing
5. **DEI analytics** — gender, ethnicity, tenure, and pay-band breakdowns by team, level, and department to support equity reporting
6. **People analytics dashboards** — attrition rates, span of control distribution, team growth trends, and open-headcount tracking
7. **CSV and API import/export** — bulk-import employee data, export org chart images (PNG/SVG) or structured data for use in presentations and reports
8. **Custom fields and org-level attributes** — extend standard fields with custom metadata (cost centre, office location, job family, performance rating)
9. **Access controls and privacy settings** — control which fields are visible to all employees vs. HR/Finance only, with role-based permissions
10. **Slack and Teams integration** — surface directory look-up and org chart navigation inside collaboration tools without leaving the app

## Competitive Differentiation Opportunities

- Affordable ChartHop alternative for mid-market companies that need live HRIS sync and headcount planning without enterprise-tier pricing
- AI-powered reorg assistant that suggests structural changes to improve span of control, reduce managerial bottlenecks, or meet headcount targets within budget constraints
- Team network graph visualisation that surfaces informal collaboration patterns (from communication metadata) alongside the formal hierarchy
- Embedded succession planning module that maps high-potential employees to open or at-risk roles, giving HR a consolidated view of bench strength
